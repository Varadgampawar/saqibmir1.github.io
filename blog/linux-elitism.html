Linux Elitism

Wed, 11 Jun 2022

In computer world,it is often said that a person who runs linux has an elitest attitude. Is there any reason behind that or is it just an ego driven emotion to tell themselves that they are smarter than others. Well to me, that is not an attitude. Yes they are the elitest. Let's find out why:

They are not afraid to stray from herd:

The fact that Linux only has 2% of the desktop operating system market share alone make the linux users the elitest because they do not care what majority of the population uses.

They are real nerds:

Even if you compare a linux user to windoze power user, he probably knows more about his operating System that the windoze power user know about his. Even an average linux user is aware about things like what a kernel is, what DE, WM, init-system user-permissions are So yeah, linux users are much more tech savvy.

They are control freaks:

Linux users like to have full control over their system. From the color of their mouse cursor to write permissions for system core configs. A windoze and a mac user cannot say the same. A linux user knows where every nut and bolt of his operating systme lies and knows how to configure them. Fr tho? How many windoze users have you seen who keep track of their config files? Heck, Do windoze programs even have config files? Wait...!. Does a windoze user even know what a config file is?

They put in the work, put in the hour:

Switching to linux is a long journey. They distro-hop, they look for alternatives for the programs that they were used to on windoze. There are some linux (arch,gentoo, lfs) users that install/compile/configure their operating system from the scratch to run their elite distro.

They are passionate:

The thing about humans is once their basic basic needs are fullfilled, if they are getting food, chothes and shelter. They start loosing that drive for survival In modern times, this is so common. We all have smartphones, access to almost everything on out fingertips, We have access to netflix, porn, we can get food on our doorsteps. All these thing keep us preoccupied so that we are never going to set foot out of out comfort zone or go out and do things that are really dificult to do.
This is the reason why so many people say linux linux is difficult. True the learning curvature is steep but that is only because we all are so used to windoze like operating systems, any other os seems so foreign. It's the passion for technology that makes a person to ditch the "out of the box" tools and try out other alternatives and choose the one that fits best according to their needs.

In conclusion: Suck my dick
